Why Phishing Works

Phishing attacks use trust, urgency, and familiar branding to trick people into revealing passwords, personal data, or financial details.

Examples of Phishing

Common phishing examples include fake login pages, spoofed URLs, fraudulent emails, and urgent security alerts that encourage hasty action.

Tips to Spot a Fake Page

Check the URL carefully, verify sender details, avoid clicking suspicious links, and never enter credentials on an untrusted site.
